《如何阅读一本书》读书笔记

一、阅读的活力

只有进行主动阅读，才谈阅读技巧，才能提高理解力。

二、阅读的层次

1.基础阅读

认字词，明字面义

2.检视阅读

在特定较短时间内掌握书中重点。

3.分析阅读

重在追寻理解，在无限时间内做最完整的阅读，直到这本书成为你自己。

4.主题阅读

总结书的架构，找出与此书主题相同的所有书，旁征博引，比较阅读。

TED-How to be a better version of yourself in times of bad

1. Treat life as a growth experience

One of the greatest days in a human being’s life on the journey of emotional maturity is the day we realize that life is not a comfort centric experience,but a growth experience.

2. Don’t focus too much on the past

Instead of just siting there and feel sorry for yourself , we should implement a zero tolerance policy on thinking of thoughts that started with “if only…”or”what if…”,because all of us can’t go back and change anything.

Free up that energy to challenge or to deal with what’s happened. Just do it now!

3. Every adversity carries with it the seed of an equivalent or greater benefit.

Most of us are so busy focused on the adversity,we don’t water the seed.

I’m a great believer that many of the gifts in life that come to us are wrapped in a thin layer of problems, probably to stop somebody else from stealing a gift. XD

22.链表中倒数第k个节点

题目描述

输入一个链表，输出该链表中倒数第k个节点。为了符合大多数人的习惯，本题从1开始计数，即链表的尾节点是倒数第1个节点。

例如，一个链表有 6 个节点，从头节点开始，它们的值依次是 1、2、3、4、5、6。这个链表的倒数第 3 个节点是值为 4 的节点。

示例：

给定一个链表: 1->2->3->4->5, 和 k = 2.

返回链表 4->5.

思路：

使用快慢指针。快指针到第k个时，慢指针在第一个；快指针到最后一个，慢指针指向倒数第k个。
